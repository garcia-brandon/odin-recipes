Mi Goreng Noodles

A noodle dish unlike any other.

Mi Goreng is a noodle dish originating in Indonesia. This dish is expected to become your new favorite meal.

Ingredients
  • Mi Goreng Instant Noodles
  • Lime
  • Boiled egg
  • Cilantro
  • Hot sauce of choice; Tapatio recommended
Steps
  1. Boil eggs to preference, peel and wash
  2. Follow instand noodle instructions
  3. Chop cilantro to preference
  4. Add egg, cilantro, and lime juice to noodles
  5. Add hot sauce
  6. Enjoy!
