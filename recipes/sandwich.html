Arugula & Roast Beef Sandwich

A sandwich that refuses to disappoint

This roast beef and arugula sandwich will leave you satiated, yet begging for more.

Ingredients
  • Arugula
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Roast Beef
  • Bread of choice; Jewish Rye is recommended
  • Cilantro
  • Dressing of choice
Steps
  1. Chop tomato, onion, and cilantro to preference
  2. Toast bread to preference
  3. Add roast beef and veggies to bread
  4. Add dressing
  5. Enjoy!
